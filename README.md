## 🎨 Website Features

### Design & UI
- **Luxury Color Palette**: Deep burgundy (#8B1538), gold (#D4AF37), soft pink (#FFB6C1), nude beige (#E5D4C1), and black
- **Advanced Animations**: Framer Motion with parallax scrolling, 3D transforms, and spring physics
- **Glassmorphism Effects**: Backdrop blur, gradient overlays, and translucent cards
- **Custom Cursor**: Animated cursor follower with magnetic effects
- **Gradient Mesh Background**: Animated floating orbs and dynamic gradients
- **Floating Particles**: Beauty icons (sparkles, stars, hearts) with physics-based animations
- **Custom Scrollbar**: Branded gold gradient scrollbar
- **Loading Screen**: Elegant pre-loader with brand animation
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with smooth breakpoints
- **Premium Typography**: Cormorant Garamond (headings) and Montserrat (body)

### Pages
- **Home** - Complete landing page with all sections
- **About** - Brand story, mission, vision, and team
- **Services** - All beauty services with detailed packages
- **Gallery** - Portfolio with filters and before/after transformations
- **Contact** - Contact form, map, hours, and booking options

- **Parallax Scrolling**: Hero section with depth-based parallax effects
